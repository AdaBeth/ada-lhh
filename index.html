About Dr Huanhuan Li

Huanhuan Li received the M.Sc. degree (Hons.) in Applied Mathematics from the School of Science in 2015 and the PhD degree (Hons.) in Traffic Information Engineering and Control from the School of Navigation in 2020, Wuhan University of Technology, Wuhan, China.
She was a visiting PhD student in Prof. Zaojian Zou’s research team from 2016 to 2018 at Shanghai Jiao Tong University and a visiting scholar in Prof. Zaili Yang’s research team from 2018 to 2019 at Liverpool John Moores University (LJMU).
She was a research fellow at Nanyang Technological University in Singapore from 2020 to 2021, whose line manager is Prof. Lam Siu Lee Jasmine.
She is currently a research fellow with the School of Engineering in LJMU, whose line manager is Prof. Zaili Yang. Her research interests include maritime big data mining, computational transportation science, maritime big data mining, intelligent transport system, maritime risk assessment, and autonomous shipping.
